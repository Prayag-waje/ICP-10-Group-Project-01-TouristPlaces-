INFORMATION ABOUT MAHARASHTRA

Maharashtra is a state in western India, known for its rich history, diverse culture, and significant economic contributions. Here are some key points about Maharashtra:
Capital: Mumbai, which is also the financial and commercial hub of India.
Language: Marathi is the official language.
Population: It is the second-most populous state in India.
Geography: Maharashtra covers a substantial portion of the Deccan Plateau and is bordered by the Arabian Sea to the west.
Economy: The state is a leader in industrial and agricultural production, trade, and transport.
Cultural Centers: Pune is known as the cultural and educational capital of the state.
